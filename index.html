About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at École Polytechnique de Montréal, under the supervision of José Fernandez (École Polytechnique) and Gianluca Stringhini (University College London).

Research interests

My research interests rely in the various aspects of the malicious infection chain, notably the detection of spam in social networks, malicious infrastructure classification through redirection chains, exploit kit and downloader analysis, and click fraud.
